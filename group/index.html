I am a firm believer that science is best done in teams, and I've been lucky enough to form a thriving research group at STScI that focuses on the study of exoplanet atmospheres.

Below you can meet the talented scientists who make up our team — those currently exploring new worlds with us, and those who have gone on to new adventures elsewhere.

Current Members

  • Amelie Gressier --- Postdoc | Research focus: exoplanet atmospheric characterization with JWST, atmospheric retrievals.
  • Måns Holmberg --- Postdoc | Research focus: exoplanet morning/evening atmospheric spectroscopy with JWST.
  • Natalie Allen --- Graduate Student | Research focus: terrestrial exoplanet atmospheric characterization with JWST.
  • Yiwei Chai --- Graduate Student | Research focus: exoplanet atmospheric characterization with both JWST and Roman.
  • Zoe Ko --- Graduate Student | Research focus: beating stellar contamination on transmission spectroscopy via Gaussian Processes.
  • Gavin Wang --- Undergraduate Student | Research focus: JWST detector modeling and exoplanet atmospheric characterization.
