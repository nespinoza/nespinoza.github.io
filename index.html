I'm Néstor Espinoza, an Assistant Astronomer (tenure-track) at the Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI). From 2017 to 2019, I was the inaugural Bernoulli Fellow and a Gruber Fellow based at the MPIA. I obtained my PhD from the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile in September 2017. My main research interests are exoplanets: from detection and characterization to how they form and what they are made of. I'm also a big statistics enthusiast and love coding.
