I'm Néstor Espinoza, a Bernoulli Fellow at the Max-Planck-Institüt für Astronomie (MPIA). I obtained my PhD from the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. My main research interests are exoplanets: from detection and characterization to how they form and what they are made of. I'm also a big statistics enthusiast and love coding.
